Who We Are

unread messages

The term Rainya is a Chakma word and stand for 2nd phase of Jum Cultivation. Similarly Tugun is stand for peak or top of the hills. The both terms comprised as Rainya Tugun is combinely used for referring the peak of the Jum field. In due honor to the culture and tradition of local ethnic communities, Rainya Tugun was adopted as a brand name in the field of tourism and hospitality.

Rainya Tugun Eco Resort is the first and pioneer eco friendly resort at large area in Rangamati district, one of the closest destinations to experience the Sub tropical paradise of Bangladesh. It is supposed to be an abode for guest in Tranquil Nature and, caters to a variety of travelers and holiday-goers who want to get away from the crowd and find shelter in the nature. however, preparatory work and the construction of infrastructure and villa is still going on and it is also going to be largest comprehensive tourist destination of CHT in future. read more

Responsible Tourism :
